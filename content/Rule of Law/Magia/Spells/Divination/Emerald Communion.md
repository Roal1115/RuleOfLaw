5th-Level Divination
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M (an emerald gemstome worth at least 500 gp)
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Number 19th of Zephyrion's great works, grand-spellcraft that elevates a wizard's connection to the weave.

Casting this spell attunes your senses to the intricate web of ley lines that permeate the world and the magic around you. For the duration, you gain the following benefits:

- You gain Arcane sight up to 120 feet, allowing you to see into the Ethereal Plane, discern illusions, shapechangers, creatures hidden by magic, and automatically identify any spells being cast.
- Additionally, you can visualize magic that is usually invisible as if it were composed of floating strands of energy, mystical glows, or arcane glyphs. This invisible magic can include that evoked by spells, magical effects, ley lines, or enchanted items.
- This sight can penetrate most barriers, but is blocked by anti-magic energy, wild magic, and lead.
- You have advantage on Intelligence (Arcana) checks to identify or discern the nature of spells and magical effects.
- If you concentrate on the spell for its full duration, after its duration has elapsed, you can change your list of prepared wizard spells. You are unable to remove this spell from your list of prepared spells through this effect. For every spell you don't change, you gain 2 temporary hit points.
- You can ritual cast this spell if you have the ritual casting feature. When casting the spell in this way, the emerald gemstone used for the casting of this spell is consumed.

```
Flavor: Juvencio respira profundamente, sintiendo el frío tacto de la esmeralda en su mano mientras sus palabras dracónicas resuenan suavemente. Al canalizar el poder del hechizo, su piel comienza a brillar con un resplandor verdoso, y sus ojos, ahora envueltos en un suave resplandor esmeralda, se abren para ver más allá del velo de la realidad.
```
