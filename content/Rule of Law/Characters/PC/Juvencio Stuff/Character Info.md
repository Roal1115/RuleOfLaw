- **Metamagic Adept**: Extended Spell, Subtle Spell.
- **Honed Divination:** Once per long rest you can cast [[Augury]] with out expending any spell slot or needing any material
- **Arcane Recover**: Once per day when you finish a short rest, you can choose expended spell slots to recover. The spell slots can have a combined level that is equal to or less than 7
- **Expert Divination**: Divination spell of 2nd level or higher using a spell slot regain one expended spell slot of lower level
- **Portentic Clairvoyance**. You can spend two of your Portent dice to cast the scrying spell without expending a spell slot or requiring any components. Additionally, this casting of the scrying spell has a casting time of 1 action instead of its original 10 minutes. Information gained through this casting is limited to visual information only.
- **The Third Eye**: one per rest choose one of this, Darkvision, Ether Plane view, Greater Comprehension or See Invisibility.
- **Chronocognizance**: Automatically know if any creature that you observe is under the haste or slow effects of the spell.
