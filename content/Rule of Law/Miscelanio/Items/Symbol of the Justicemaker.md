Name: "[[Symbol of the Justicemaker]]"
Attunement: Yes
---
___
- An ancient holy symbol, theorized to be used by dragon clerics of Bahamut. It has appearance of a small dragon claw made of platinum
- The claw has 3 charges
- While wearing it, you can as an action and expend 1 charge to cast one of the following spells at their base level:
	- Guiding bolt (1st level)
	- Calm emotions (2nd level)
	- Tongues (3rd level)
- Additionally, if you're a cleric or paladin affiliated with Bahamut or the God of Tyr, you can expend 3 charges to regain a use of your channel divinity feature
- The claw regains 1d3 charges daily at dawn
