---
Name: "[[Sweet Tooths Golden Arm]]"
Attunement: Yes
---
___
- Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement)
- A very small dessert fork that seems to be made from some gold-like metal. Non-magical material stabbed with this fork is edible and digestible to you while attuned, letting you take small, elegant nibbles out of seemingly any material.
- One could use the fork to take out bites out of a prison wall, eventually opening an escape, or out of a lockbox's inferior, cracking it open.
- The fork is small, even for a child's standards, so its pickings can be no bigger than a pea, even still, with enough patience and a tenacious stomach one could devour a fortress. Material eaten with this item can be magically digested and gives sustenance, albeit gains an extremely sweet flavor, but must still be digested as normal.
