___
When you attune to this Materia, you gain access to a repertoire of additional spells. Each of this spells count as prepared spell for you, but it doesnt count againts the number of spells you can prepare. The Materia Arcana contains the following spells: [[Identify]], [[Detect Magic]], [[Mage Armor]], [[Magic Missile]], [[Mirror Image]], [[Misty Step]], [[Counterspell]], [[Fly]], [[Lightning Bolt]], [[Fire Shield]], [[Cone of Cold]]
